Social Impact


Empowering the community by creating means and opportunity for social growth of society.

Impacting Communities

We at Bykea aim to empower the community and expand the horizons of business in Pakistan by eliminating barriers faced by the common people and small business owners while trying to touch and improve as many lives in Pakistan to create a stronger community and impact society on a large scale.

The Challenge

In Pakistan, finding good healthcare is hard therefore, a lot of people avoid visiting the hospital for a check-up. Visiting a healthcare professional can be expensive for someone who does not live close to a hospital and waiting for a doctor takes away a lot of the time.

Our Goal

A healthy population plays a large role in social and national development. We want to promote healthcare within Pakistan to decrease absenteeism and build a more productive workforce in the country.

The Solution

We have partnered up with healthcare professionals to offer at-home services to people who are unable to make it to the hospital. Our Bykea Mart service allows people to receive their pharmaceutical needs right to their doorstep.
